Privacy Policy

Hashtag Copier for Instagram does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

The extension only reads publicly visible hashtag text from Instagram posts and reels when the user explicitly clicks the extension button.

No personal information, account data, private messages, browsing history, or payment data is collected or transmitted.

This extension operates entirely locally in the user's browser.

This extension is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to Instagram or Meta.
